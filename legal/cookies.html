Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device on future visits and remember information such as your language preferences or login status.
When you first visit a website, the web server sends a cookie to your browser, which stores it. On subsequent visits, your browser sends the cookie back to the server, allowing the website to recognize you and retain your settings.

What and which cookies do I use?

I use cookies to:
  • Ensure the functionality and security of the website: These cookies are essential for core features such as navigation, login, shopping cart, and to protect against security threats.
  • Improve your user experience: By remembering your preferences (such as language, region, or display settings), cookies help personalize your experience and make the website easier to use.
  • Analyze and optimize website usage: Analytics cookies collect anonymized data about how visitors interact with the site, which helps me understand usage patterns, identify popular content, and improve the website’s performance and structure.
  • Provide personalized content and advertising: Cookies enable the delivery of tailored content and advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior, both on this website and across other platforms.
  • Enable third-party features and integrations: Some cookies are set by third-party providers to support features like embedded videos, maps, or social media sharing, and may also be used for additional analytics or advertising purposes.
Types of cookies I use:
  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functions of the website and cannot be disabled. They ensure security functions and enable navigation on the website.
  • Preferences and Personalization Cookies: These cookies enable the website to remember choices you have made (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Analytics and Statistic Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use my website. They help me understand which pages are most popular and how visitors navigate the website. All data collected is anonymized.
  • Marketing and Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to show visitors relevant advertising on other websites. They are also used to limit the frequency of ad display and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by third-party services that I use to provide additional features, such as embedded videos, maps, or social media content. Third-party providers may also use these cookies to deliver personalized advertising.

Managing your cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time on any of my pages by clicking on “Change Cookie Preferences” at the bottom of the page and adjusting and saving your preferred settings. Alternatively, you can also manage the cookies on my website(s) via your browser and allow or block certain types of cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of the website.
The use of cookies is in accordance with applicable data protection laws, particularly the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Essential cookies are based on our legitimate interest to operate the website, while all others require your explicit consent. For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
