Why Choose Shoply?

Cloud Synchronization

Access your shopping lists from any device. Your data is automatically synced across all your devices in real-time.

Real-time Collaboration

Share lists with family and friends. Edit together in real-time and see changes instantly as they happen.

Offline Availability

Create and manage local lists that work without internet. Perfect for when you're on the go or have limited connectivity.

Smart Categories

Organize your items with custom categories. Keep your shopping organized and efficient with smart grouping.

Import & Export

Easily import existing lists or export your data. Full control over your shopping list data with convenient backup options.

Discord Integration

Sign in with your Discord account for seamless authentication and easy sharing with your Discord communities.

Chrome & Edge Installation on Desktop

1

Open Shoply in Chrome/Edge

Navigate to the Shoply web app in your Chrome or Edge browser. Make sure you're on the main app page.

2

Look for the Install Icon

In the address bar, you'll see a small install icon ( or ). Click on it.

If you don't see the install icon, try refreshing the page or check if your browser supports PWAs.
3

Click "Install"

A popup will appear asking if you want to install Shoply. Click "Install" to proceed.

4

Launch the App

After installation, Shoply will open in its own window. You can now access it from your desktop or start menu.

Congratulations! Shoply is now installed as a PWA on your device.

Firefox Installation

1

Open Shoply in Firefox

2
